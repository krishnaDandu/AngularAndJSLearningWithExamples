ABOUT US

The story of a house-wife (Chitra aka Aunty) from Patna who started pursuing her passion of cooking in her second innings, at the age of 65.

With support of family, she started her home cloud kitchen (Aunty's Kitchen) in her society in Bangalore. And the culinary journey began!

Delicacies made by her received huge admiration and love from the residents (Bestseller being Litti Chokha). Aunty's Kitchen was operational twice in a week and was going good.

But life had different plans. There were frequent health issues and the kitchen remained closed for many months. But as we know, a woman (mother) doesn't give up easily. Infact, she stood tall and came back stronger with a more structured way!

Then, was the inception of Litti In Town (LIT) in 2023, an initiative by Aunty's Kitchen to take the famous Litti Chokha to another level.

We have introduced different variants of Litti in both Veg and Non-Veg category.

Ingredients used are fresh and of premium quality (be it Atta, Oil, Ghee, Sattu, veggies etc.) - even meat used is fresh (No Frozen items). We strictly follow - NO compromise on quality as Health is priority.

Mantra is to provide homely, healthy and hygenic food so that everyone can relish.
